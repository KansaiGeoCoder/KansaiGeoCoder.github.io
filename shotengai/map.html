About the Shotengai Atlas

The Shotengai Atlas maps Japan’s traditional shopping streets — the Shotengai — as living spaces of daily culture and urban life.

Each line represents a shopping street, showing its location, structure, length, and status. Together, these features reveal how local communities sustain walkable city spaces across Japan.

Purpose

  • Preserve the cultural and spatial identity of Shotengai
  • Support revitalization through accessible spatial data
  • Connect research, associations, and the public

Data

Data combines fieldwork, open sources, and community input and is stored in a Supabase database. Geometry is served as GeoJSON for fast rendering.

Project by Maximilian Prutsch — ongoing, experimental, and collaborative.
